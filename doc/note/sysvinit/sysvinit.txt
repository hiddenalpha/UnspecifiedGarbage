
System V init
=============

Create/update scripts in "/etc/init.d/".



## Enable / Disable service

  update-rc.d YOURSVC defaults
  update-rc.d YOURSVC remove



## Start / stop service

  /etc/init.d/YOURSVC start
  /etc/init.d/YOURSVC stop
